MY RECEIPES

Dosa

★★★★★
20 min • Serves 2 • 180 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 cup rice
  • ½ cup urad dal
  • ½ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
Instructions
  1. Soak rice, dal, and fenugreek seeds for 6 hours.
  2. Grind into smooth batter.
  3. Ferment overnight.
  4. Spread batter thinly on hot pan.
  5. Cook until crisp and golden.

Idli

★★★★★
15 min • Serves 3 • 120 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 cups idli batter
  • Oil for greasing molds
Instructions
  1. Grease idli plates with oil.
  2. Pour batter into molds.
  3. Steam for 12–15 minutes.
  4. Cool slightly and remove.
  5. Serve with chutney or sambar.

Hyderabadi Biryani

★★★★★
45 min • Serves 4 • 420 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500 g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Fried onions
  • Biryani spices
Instructions
  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook rice until 70% done.
  3. Layer rice and chicken in pot.
  4. Seal and cook on low heat (dum).
  5. Garnish and serve hot.

Upma

★★★★☆
20 min • Serves 2 • 250 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 cup rava (semolina)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Mustard seeds & curry leaves
  • 2 cups water
Instructions
  1. Dry roast rava and keep aside.
  2. Prepare tempering with mustard seeds.
  3. Add onions and vegetables.
  4. Add water and bring to boil.
  5. Stir in rava and cook till fluffy.
